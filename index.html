Steal the Egg

  • High Concept: Bird Egg thiefs at work
  • Player Experience Goals: I wanted players to understand the pain that follows stealing the precious eggs of large birds.
  • Genre: Asteroids
  • Game play: You can move around with the arrow keys within the shown space. Objective is to avoid the birds.
  • Prototype goal: I wanted to test Bird AI targetting the player.
  • Features: Poor sprites, no functioning game play
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is a field with birds. You, the player, have stolen an egg from some birds and they are angry
  • Target Audience: Asteroids with birds?
  • Play time: No end game made
  • Strategies: Run away!
  • Prototype Assets: I made all of my assets.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Asteroids. I borrowed code from both the Tanks and Starstruck examples.
